Annual Change Drive/Adopt-a-Family

Each class is collecting change in support of 3 Navajo families. The class that collects the most change will win a class party. All the money collected will be used to purchase items such as clothing and toys for these families. There is also a tree in the front office with specific items needed written on an ornament. Please contact any PTO member with any questions.

Harkins 2011 Cups and Passes

How about some easy holiday gifts! We will be selling Harkins Movie Cups for $4 and gift cards are $10. We will continue to sell until supplies run out. Send in the form or stop by the front office.

Help Navajo with a Tax Credit Donation

Did you know that Arizona law allows taxpayers to receive a tax credit of up to $200 for individual filers and up to $400 for married filers for donations made to SUSD schools? You can claim the tax credit when filing your 2010 Arizona State Income Tax return. The school must receive the funds by December 31, 2010. It's a win, win situation for you and the school!

Navajo Celebrates 50 Years!
