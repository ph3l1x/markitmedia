Skateland

Navajo and Pueblo are holding a joint skating party on January 26th at Skateland from 2 – 5 pm. Look for the flyers coming home. This is a really fun event with tug of war contests and other games.

Navajo Turns 50!

Everyone is invited to come join the fun at our 50th Anniversary Celebration on Saturday, February 12th from 6 to 8 pm. There will be music, dancing, food, a Silent Auction and reminiscing.

Chess Sets Needed

After-school clubs are beginning on Tuesday, February 1st. Chess Club is hoping to get a few chess sets donated for their use. If you are able to donate one please drop off at the front office.

Auction Donations Needed

Each class is responsible for putting together a basket for our auction. We also look for donations from Navajo families and the community. If you work for a company willing to donate a gift card or a service to the auction, we would greatly appreciate it.

Space and Science Night

Navajo's yearly Space and Science Night is quickly approaching so mark those calendars for Thursday, February 10th 6 - 8 pm. This community event will include telescope viewing with Project Astro from U of A and a Star Lab, ASU's Deadalus Astronautic Club and water bottle launching (bring a 1-2 liter bottle), ASU's Colleges of Sustainability and Human Evolution and Change, SNAP-ED Nutritional Program, Embry Riddle Aeronautical University, Liberty Wildlife and many more hands-on science explorations.

String Sounds Assembly

On January 6th the Pre-K through 6th grade saw a special presentation of the String Sounds performing arts program. Students listened to a string quartet while actors/dancers performed the fairytale "The Magic Medallion."
