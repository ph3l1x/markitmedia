RoboBuffs Compete in Regional FLL

Navajo RoboBuffs, for the third time, went to the Regional First Lego League competition Saturday at Supai Middle School. This year's challenge was even more difficult than last year's. The team "invented" a small scanner meant to use infrared light to constantly scan a kitchen in a restaurant or small shop. They also wrote a song and sang to the tune of Eye of the Tiger, explaining the research they did together. Our RoboBuffs walked away with the award for Teamwork within the area of the FLL Core Values! Big thanks to Coach Cathy White for her leadership and stamina! BTW: Navajo's first robotics team, now 8th graders at Mohave, won overall Grand Champions and will go on to State!

Help Navajo with a Tax Credit Donation

Did you know that Arizona law allows taxpayers to receive a tax credit of up to $200 for individual filers and up to $400 for married filers for donations made to SUSD schools? You can claim the tax credit when filing your 2011 Arizona State Income Tax return. The school must receive the funds by December 31, 2011. It's a win, win situation for you and the school! Consult your tax professional.
