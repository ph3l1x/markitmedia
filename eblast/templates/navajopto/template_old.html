November Election

The School Bond is not the only major issue on the November 2nd ballot. In addition to the many positions up for election in our state government, there are two open seats on the Scottsdale School Board. The three candidates are Pam Kirby, Denny Brown and incumbent Dieter Schaefer. The School Board's decisions have a DAILY IMPACT on our children. Click on each of their names to look at their information and answers to some questions at the following website http://www.azcentral.com/news/election/questionnaire2010/schoolboard.php

Fall Festival

Fall Festival this year is on Saturday, October 23rd, and is a great family and community event. Parents talk to your homeroom parent about your child's classroom project for Fall Festival. There will also be sign-ups coming home asking for volunteers at the game booths and for candy/cake donations.

For the second year, the festival will include a "craft fair". If you are a crafter, have a home-based business (i.e. Gold Canyon Candles, Avon etc...) or a local business owner looking to get the word out, you can rent a space for the evening for either $20 or by donating an item worth at least $20 to our Silent Auction. If you are interested please contact Missy at 602-919-5251.

Do You Shop at Fresh and Easy?

Fresh and Easy has begun a Shop for Schools Program. For every receipt with a minimum of $20 F&E will give $1 back to our school. Please put your receipts in the box by the PTO bulletin board in the front office or send in with your child. The collection period runs Sept. 15th to Dec. 31st.
