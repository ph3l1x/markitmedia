The Desert Tortoise Habitat is Underway

Mr. Loucks, some of his students, Mrs. Campbell and some parent volunteers have begun construction of the desert tortoise habitat! Half of the butterfly garden has been designated as the home for our desert tortoise which will be arriving in early October. The enclosure will include a retaining wall, a winter den, a summer den, and some native plants for the tortoise to munch on.
