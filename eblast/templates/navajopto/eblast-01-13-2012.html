Adopt-a-Family/Change Drive

Congratulations to Ms. Hellring's class for raising the most money in our annual Change Drive! Her class brought in $128.86 and will get a pizza party. The grand total raised was $680, which was used to purchase gifts, clothes and grocery cards for 3 families that the PTO adopted.

Got Juice?

Mrs. Johnson (PTO Treasurer) has been juicing cucumbers, kale, parsley and beets from the Navajo gardens along with apples and pineapples for many of the classes. The kids have been great sports about trying the bright pink and/or green concoctions while learning about nutrition and sampling veggies many of them have never tried before!

Used Book Sale

Making room for all the new items you acquired over the holidays? Check your closets and bookshelves and keep sending in books of all types for our used book sale which will take place in May. This was a very successful event last year that both raised much needed money and got books into the hands of eager readers for the summer at great prices.

Congratulations Cristi Youngkin!

Mrs. Youngkin, Kindergarten teacher, recently passed the National Board Certification exam. This is one of the highest levels of certification that a teacher can obtain. It is an extremely rigorous process with fewer than 5% of teachers nationwide achieving this distinction!
