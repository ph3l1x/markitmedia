CATALOG FUNDRAISER

This is our biggest fundraiser of the year. We have a new company this year called MrZ/Red Apple. You can email your out-of-town family and they can order online. Get your Christmas shopping done early! You can find great deals on magazines , order a wide variety of food products and find fun items for the home. Online there are more items available. With your support we hope to continue free after-school clubs and support the Navajo gardens and tortoise habitat. Orders are due 9/12 and please check that you've collected all the money. Thank you.

ICE CREAM SOCIAL & PTO General Meeting

Come meet other Navajo families at this annual event being held Thursday, September 15th in the cafeteria from 6 – 7 pm. PTO will be serving free ice cream cones! This is also a great opportunity to meet the PTO board, ask questions about what PTO does, and also sign up for one of our many committees.

CLASSES FOR PARENTS

Every Wednesday, starting on 9/14 parenting classes will be held in Room 13 from 2 -3 pm. Beginning Sept 15th English will be offered every Thursday for parents in the library from 11 to noon. Both classes run through April. For more information or to sign up please call Elizabeth Kloehr at 480-484-2619.

COFFEE WITH SHAUN

Come join Principal Holmes in the teacher lounge on Friday, Sept. 23rd at 9am. Coffee, juice and snacks will be served. There is no set agenda, it's just a chance to get to know Navajo's principal a little better and ask questions about our school and our district.

VISION AND HEARING, Tuesday 9/27

Every year all the students at Navajo undergo a vision and hearing screening. We are still looking for more volunteers to help out. The screening lasts all day but if it's OK if you are only able to help a few hours. PTO will be offering lunch for the volunteers. Please email Katy Foster at jfoster19@cox.net if you are able to help.
