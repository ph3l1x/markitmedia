Greening Up Navajo!

Cisco donated some beautiful shrubs and trees to Navajo. Parents. kids and Cisco employees helped plant the first delivery during fall break. These low water plants will really help beautify Navajo. Thank you Cisco for the lasting donation.

We have also had a wonderful donation from Braden's Garden. Our site, Braden's Garden II, includes 8 raised garden beds. Students have built the beds and filled them with garden soil. We hope to add compost soon and begin planting.

Our traditional vegetable garden has become a pumpkin patch! Last year's departing 6th graders planted a pumpkin patch for the new kindergarten kids. Pumpkins are ready for harvest just in time for the Kinder's fall harvest unit! PTO donated the materials for an automatic watering system, which the 6th graders help install last spring so the pumpkins would get water over the summer.

The Butterfly Garden has had lots of butterflies visiting over the last few weeks. Kids have been helping weed the space and we will be replacing plants this month.

Math and Technology Night is Nov. 4th!

Navajo Math and Technology Night this school year will focus on real life measuring with hands-on experiences for students using measuring tapes, rulers, yardsticks, cups, spoons, scales, calculators, protractors, and thermometers. Parents and students will also learn about school fusion, how to get student grades and assignments on-line (grades 4-6), district resources, e-books, and robotics demonstrations.
