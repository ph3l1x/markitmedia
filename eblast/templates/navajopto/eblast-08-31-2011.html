THANK YOU

Thank you to all the parents who signed-up for one of our many events and programs. PTO couldn't do it without all our volunteers!

SKATELAND

Come enjoy an afternoon of fun and get to know other Navajo families. Our first skate party of the year is Wed. August 31st from 2:30 to 5 pm and will have a LUAU theme. Flyers with coupons have been sent home with all students.

CATALOG FUNDRAISER

This is our biggest fundraiser of the year. We have a new company this year called MrZ/Red Apple. Catalogs were sent home today and you can order online as well! Get your Christmas shopping done early! You can find great deals on magazines , order a wide variety of food products and find fun items for the home. With your support we hope to continue free after-school clubs and support the Navajo gardens and tortoise habitat.

BUZZBERRY THURSDAYS

Come out and support Navajo and a local business at the same time. We are resuming the sale of smoothies on Thursdays after school beginning Sept. 1st. They are available at parent pick-up for $3.

BUILDING MATERIALS NEEDED

We are building a desert tortoise habitat and would love to get some of the materials donated. Some of the items we need are rebar, block, cement, mortar and wire rolls. Please contact Sara Johnson at (480) 217-3707.
