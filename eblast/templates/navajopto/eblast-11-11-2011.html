Math Night

Several hundred people attended Navajo's annual Math Night. Participants filled their "Math Night Passport" with stamps by going to each activity station. Students measured ingredients to make GORP and playdough, played chess and Blokus, tried their hand at Sudoku, played card games and dominos. It was a fun night for all.

It's a Girl!

Navajo's tortoise has arrived. Students voted and chose Tortilla as her name. She has moved into her habitat, tested out the dens, nibbled on her favorite flowers and has gone into hibernation.
