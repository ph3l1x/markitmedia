Fall Festival and Craft Fair, Sat Oct. 22nd 5 to 8 pm

We will be selling tickets and bracelets at Parent pick up the week of Fall Festival 10/19-10/21. Tickets for food and games are 4 for $1. Ride bracelets are $7 presale and $8 the night of the event and raffle tickets are $1. If you link your Fry's card (see below) and bring in the receipt you can get 4 FREE TICKETS!

We need your help to make Fall Festival a success!

Look out for the flyers coming home about this fun annual event. We are looking for lots of volunteers to help, set up, clean up and work the event. We also appreciate donations of candy for game prizes and baked goods for the cake walk. We are also seeking donations from our community to include in the Auction/Raffle. This is our best attended event of the year and offers great exposure for businesses. If you know a business interested in donating a service, product or gift card, please contact Sara Johnson at (480) 217-3707 or sdcphx@live.com by Oct. 20th.

Attention Crafters and Home-based Businesses

This is our third year to include a craft fair with our Fall Festival. If you are interested in selling a craft or product, or offering a service you can rent a table for $20 or we will waive the fee if you donate an item valued at $20 or more for our auction/raffle. Please contact Dennis Enriquez at 602-570-1262.
