Time Capsule Dedication

On December 15th, the entire student body gathered on the basketball courts to honor the school's 50th anniversary. Each class put something they had selected into the time capsule and also made up a cheer. Afterwards, the BMX bikers performed their stunts and even jumped over Mrs. Carroll; her class was the top fundraiser in the Great American Fundraiser.

Navajo Vegetable Gardens Update

Over winter break, the first step in an automatic watering system was installed, so that the 9 vegetable beds could be set on a consistent schedule. As you pick up or drop off your child(ren) stop by the gardens on Camelback and check out the abundant kale, broccoli, spinach, bok choy and lettuce. Mrs. Frailey's class has been preparing delicious salads from the gardens as part of their science unit "Structures of Life."

White Elephant Gift Exchange

The next PTO General Meeting will be January 20th at 6pm in the Teacher's Lounge. We will be hosting a White Elephant Gift Exchange and have some snacks. If you wish to participate please bring an unwanted gift from around your house. It should be wrapped but have no tag. Unfamiliar with a White Elephant Exchange? It's just a fun way to "recycle" gifts! It can be a funny/silly item or it can simply be an item you no longer want or need. Use your imagination!

Three Cheers for Mrs. Ahmad's Class!

Mrs. Ahmad's class held a coin drive that raised $25 at the urging of classmate Felina Hans. Felina had written a heartfelt letter about the African village of Bulembu and the many orphans there. The money will be sent to Partners in Action and used towards the purchase a cow.
