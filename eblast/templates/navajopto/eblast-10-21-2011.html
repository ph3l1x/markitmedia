Spread the word and come join us for this fun community event. Tickets for food and games are 4 for $1. Ride bracelets are $7 presale and $8 the night of the event and raffle tickets are $1. Fresh and Easy Shopping Night, Oct. 26th 4-8 pm

Fresh and Easy's Shop for Schools Program is sponsoring a Shopping Night for Navajo Wednesday, October 26th from 4 to 8 pm. We will get 5% of all the sales generated during that time. Please come out and do your shopping at the Thomas and Hayden store and help support Navajo! This is in addition to the $1 we get for every receipt with a minimum of $20! Please put your receipts in the box in the front office or send in with your child.
